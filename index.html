Benefits of Ayurveda

Holistic Approach

Ayurveda considers the entire well-being, addressing root causes, while Western medicine often focuses on symptoms, offering comprehensive care for lasting health

Ayurveda utilizes herbs and lifestyle adjustments, minimizing side effects, whereas Western medicine often employs pharmaceuticals with potential adverse reactions

Natural Healing

Ayurveda tailors treatments based on individual constitutions, promoting personalized healthcare, while Western medicine often follows standardized approaches for broader populations.
